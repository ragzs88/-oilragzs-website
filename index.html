The Story
"Oil Ragzs came to me on the road — hauling crude through the oil patch, alone in the cab. The idea just hit me one day while I was out there driving and working.."

Every piece is designed around what work actually demands — oil field, rail yard, ag equipment, construction site, or the road hauling it all. Oil Ragzs is built for the hard working person, first and always.
